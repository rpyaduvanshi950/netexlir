# Goal
Build an AI-assisted ecommerce forecasting utility that predicts future revenue
and ROAS as PROBABILISTIC RANGES (not single numbers), for 30/60/90-day windows,
from historical marketing + sales data (GA4 source/medium + Shopify, covering
Google/Meta/MS Ads). It must also: accept future media-budget inputs and simulate
the effect on revenue/ROAS, and use an LLM to explain forecasts, flag anomalies,
and surface operational risks. Use the attribution in the data AS-IS — do NOT build
a custom attribution engine or full Media Mix Model. Forecasts are aggregate-period,
not daily.

# Work in strict phases. Stop and summarize after each phase before moving on.
